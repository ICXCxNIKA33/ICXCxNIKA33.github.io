Portfolio Website Proposal

Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to act as my portfolio, a document I will show to my potential employers to demonstrate my skill, work, and achivements. As someone in the field of software development and analysis, 

Target Audience

my main target audience will be those interested in devloping new software, such as front-end developers, app developers,or people looking for personal websites. In addition, I will also be targeting employers involved in the more technical side of software, such as IT organizations, cybersecurity, and back-end developers  

Portfolio Components Research

To create my portfolio, I will need the some of the following elements. first, my portfolio materials which consist of my introduction -- a summary about myself and my work, my professional experience and education -- such as school and work exepirience, my accomplishments and awards -- things like scholarships or testimonials, and samples of my work -- only selecting the most important and relevant ones(Indeed). I will also need to include project descriptions for my work, that explain my role and contributions, the tools and technologies I used, and visuals like links to my work. I will need to make sure that my portfolio is well designed using simple fonts and neutral color schemes in addition to mobile friendly formatting. I will need supporting documents like a short bio, personal statement and contact info. And finaly, I will need to make sure that my portfolio is organized logically, organizing projects by necessary skill, by project type, or chronologically(meritamerica).
